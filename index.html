Islands to Visit

Maui

The Valley Isle

Maui is a great place to explore when visiting Hawaii. With the Molokini Crater just off of the coast and a beautiful volcano to explore, this island is a must-see!

Oahu

The Gathering Place

Oahu is home to a majority of Hawaii Residents. Come here to enjoy true Hawaiian culture while also seeing some of the natural beauty that the island has to offer!

Kauai

The Garden Isle

With beautiful green landscapes, it is no surprise that this island is nicknamed the garden island. Come explore Kauai by going on beatiful hikes through the mountain ranges!

All ages!

November - April

Snorkeling

Ages 7 and older
Come see some of the most diverse marine life in the entire world! The water in Hawaii is pretty calm, so grab your snorkel gear and see marine life like you have never seen it before.
All year round

Foods to Try

Poke

A local favorite

If raw fish is your thing, you have to try poke! It is typically served in a bowl with other fresh foods, such as avacadoes and lettuce! Although the ingredients are simple, combined they create a poweful and delicious flavor.

Manapua

A delicious meat-filled pastry

Similar to a Kolache or baozi, manapua is something that you have to experience while on the island! It is a hawaiian roll that is stuffed with pork or other savory fillings.
